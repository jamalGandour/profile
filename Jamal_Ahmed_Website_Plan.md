# Jamal Ahmed Website and Business Plan

## Positioning

Jamal Ahmed should be positioned as a senior supply chain consultant who connects practical operational experience with modern AI, data analytics, dashboards, automation, AI agents, and professional web systems.

Primary title:

**Supply Chain Consultant & AI Solutions Specialist**

## Core Message

More than 25 years of supply chain experience, supported by modern AI and data capabilities, to help organizations reduce inventory, improve planning accuracy, reduce waste, develop reports, build smart systems, train teams, and apply AI tools effectively in business operations.

## Target Audience

- FMCG companies and other operational businesses
- Manufacturing, distribution, retail, and trading companies
- Supply chain managers and teams
- Business owners who need dashboards, reports, automation, or websites
- Professionals who want practical training in supply chain, AI, and data tools
- Institutions that need educational websites or digital learning platforms
