# Website Feature Specification for AI Builders

This document can be given to Claude, Base44, Lovable, v0, or another AI builder to create a similar professional website and admin platform.

## Project Name

Jamal Ahmed Professional Profile Platform

## Main Objective

Build a professional personal-brand and consulting website for a Supply Chain and AI Transformation Consultant.

The website should present consulting services, AI solutions, training programs, blog content, downloadable resources, case studies, and a secure admin dashboard for content management, backup, and restore.

## Brand Positioning

Jamal Ahmed is a Supply Chain Consultant and AI Solutions Specialist with more than 25 years of experience in supply chain management, FMCG operations, logistics, warehousing, transportation, fleet operations, inventory control, procurement, demand planning, dashboards, automation, and AI-enabled business systems.

The website should position him as:

- Supply Chain AI Transformation Consultant
- Practical AI implementation advisor for business teams
- Supply chain performance improvement expert
- Training designer and professional learning platform creator
- Dashboard, automation, and business systems builder

## Target Audience

- FMCG companies

## Data Safety Requirements

Important:

Do not track the live SQLite database in Git.

Use environment variable:

```text
DB_PATH=/home/YOUR_USER/profile-data/jamal-profile.db
```

Production database should live outside GitHub deployment folder.

Recommended Hostinger structure:

```text
/home/YOUR_USER/profile-data/jamal-profile.db
/home/YOUR_USER/profile-data/backups/
```

Add `.gitignore` rules:

```text
data/*.db
data/*.db-*
data/*.sqlite
data/*.sqlite-*
*.db-wal
*.db-shm
```
