# AI Tools for Managers Guide

Use AI tools to improve productivity, reporting, analysis, and decision support. Keep final business decisions under human ownership.

## High-Value Use Cases
- Summarize reports and meeting notes
- Draft SOPs and process documents
- Analyze KPI exceptions
- Build Excel formulas and dashboard logic
- Create training outlines and quizzes
- Draft customer and supplier communication
- Generate improvement action plans

## Supply Chain Use Cases
- Inventory risk explanation
- Forecast assumption review
- Slow-moving stock action suggestions
- Delivery follow-up message drafting
- Supplier performance summary
- Warehouse process improvement ideas

## Practical Prompt Structure
1. Define the role: "Act as a supply chain performance consultant."
2. Provide business context.
3. Provide the data or issue.
4. Ask for structured output.
5. Request risks, KPIs, actions, and next steps.

## Governance
- Do not upload confidential data without approval.
- Validate AI output before use.
- Keep human approval for business decisions.
- Create standard prompts for repeated work.
- Train teams on quality review and limitations.
