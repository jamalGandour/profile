# Demand Planning Improvement Framework

Use this framework to improve forecast discipline and planning accountability.

## 1. Baseline
- Collect historical sales by SKU, customer, channel, and period
- Identify abnormal demand
- Separate regular demand, promotions, tenders, and one-time orders

## 2. Forecast Review
- Review forecast accuracy and bias
- Capture assumptions from sales and marketing
- Add customer and market intelligence
- Document exceptions and overrides

## 3. Planning Rhythm
- Weekly short-term review
- Monthly demand review
- S&OP alignment with supply, finance, and commercial teams
- Clear approval process for major changes

## 4. Dashboard
- Forecast accuracy
- Bias
- Top forecast errors
- Stockout risk
- Excess stock risk
- Service level impact

## 5. Improvement Actions
- Assign owner per exception
- Track repeated forecast errors
- Review promotion performance
- Improve master data and planning parameters
- Train teams on AI-assisted analysis and structured assumptions
