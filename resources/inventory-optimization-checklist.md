# Inventory Optimization Checklist

Use this checklist to identify practical opportunities to reduce inventory while protecting service level.

## Stock Segmentation
- Classify items by ABC value
- Add demand frequency and criticality
- Separate active, slow-moving, obsolete, and seasonal stock
- Identify strategic safety stock items

## Risk Review
- Shortage risk by SKU
- Excess stock by SKU
- Expiry exposure
- Aging stock
- Items with no demand in recent periods

## Policy Review
- Minimum and maximum stock levels
- Reorder points
- Safety stock assumptions
- Supplier lead-time reliability
- Order frequency and MOQ impact

## Planning Review
- Forecast accuracy by item group
- Demand exceptions
- Promotion and event assumptions
- Sales and operations alignment
- Manual overrides and approval rules

## Action Plan
- Reduce excess stock by priority
- Protect high-service items
- Review slow-moving items with sales
- Build weekly exception dashboard
- Assign owners and target dates
