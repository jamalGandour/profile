# Supply Chain KPI Checklist

Use this checklist to structure a leadership-ready supply chain performance review.

## Service
- Case fill rate
- On-time delivery
- Order cycle time
- Customer complaints
- Backorders and service failures

## Inventory
- Inventory value
- Inventory days of supply
- Slow-moving and obsolete stock
- Expiry exposure
- Stockout rate
- Inventory accuracy

## Planning
- Forecast accuracy
- Forecast bias
- Demand exceptions
- Plan adherence
- Promotion and seasonality review

## Warehouse
- Picking accuracy
- Receiving cycle time
- Dispatch accuracy
- Space utilization
- Damage and handling losses

## Transport and Fleet
- On-time dispatch
- Delivery cost per case or order
- Vehicle utilization
- Fuel performance
- Route adherence

## Procurement
- Supplier service level
- Purchase price variance
- Supplier lead time
- Supplier quality issues
- Contract compliance

## Management Routine
- Weekly KPI review
- Clear owner per KPI
- Action log for exceptions
- Root-cause analysis for repeated issues
- Monthly improvement roadmap
